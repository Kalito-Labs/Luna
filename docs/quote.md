### Ralph Waldo Emerson

1. “To be yourself in a world that is constantly trying to make you something else is the greatest accomplishment.”
2. “What lies behind us and what lies before us are tiny matters compared to what lies within us.”
3. “The only person you are destined to become is the person you decide to be.”
4. “Do not go where the path may lead, go instead where there is no path and leave a trail.”
5. “Adopt the pace of nature: her secret is patience.”
6. “Make the most of yourself, for that is all there is of you.”
7. “Nothing great was ever achieved without enthusiasm.”

### Henry David Thoreau

8. “What you get by achieving your goals is not as important as what you become by achieving your goals.”
9. “It’s not what you look at that matters, it’s what you see.”
10. “Go confidently in the direction of your dreams. Live the life you have imagined.”

### Marcus Aurelius

11. “You have power over your mind—not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength.”
12. “The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts.”
13. “Waste no more time arguing about what a good man should be. Be one.”

### Seneca

14. “We suffer more often in imagination than in reality.”
15. “True happiness is to enjoy the present, without anxious dependence upon the future.”
16. “He who is brave is free.”

### Lao Tzu

17. “A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.”
18. “Mastering others is strength; mastering yourself is true power.”
19. “Nature does not hurry, yet everything is accomplished.”

### Confucius

20. “It does not matter how slowly you go as long as you do not stop.”
21. “Wherever you go, go with all your heart.”

### Epictetus

22. “It’s not what happens to you, but how you react to it that matters.”
23. “No man is free who is not master of himself.”
24. “Make the best use of what is in your power, and take the rest as it happens.”

### Rumi

25. “Yesterday I was clever, so I wanted to change the world. Today I am wise, so I am changing myself.”
